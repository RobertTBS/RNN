Quickly Mom put everything back where they should be before it fell. Then she glared at the kids. Her glare always put a shiver up the kids spine.“No more sugar puffs for the rest of the week,” Mom told them.

“What?!?!?! Already?!?! We had five five gallon boxes!!!’’ She sounded very, very, angry. She walked upstairs and shook her head. “Too many sugar puffs. Too many.’’
