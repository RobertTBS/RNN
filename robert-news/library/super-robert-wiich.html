He brought the sides up high and slammed the masher down hundreds of times in a few measly seconds.


Robert scraped off the potato guts and looked down at the potatoes. They were very mashed, so Robert dumped in more, as mashed potatoes and gravy are very delicious. He repeated the mashing process until it was perfectly mashed up.


Meanwhile, the others were finishing up the movie. They walked out and pulled up the sound screen, which Robert would have to have been the one that put it down. They were suddenly attracted to the smell of cooking turkey.


The girls and parents suddenly walked in on Robert, seeming to be attracted to Robert’s cooking turkey.


They put their hands to the glass and licked their lips, seeming to taste the turkey.


Robert shooed them away and locked the door to keep them out of the way.


Robert put the potatoes in the warmer to keep warm. Then, he pulled out his box of 10 person stuffing. This one he specially prepared for him. The box for everyone else was handled with mostly good care.


Robert prepared everything else essential for the meal, including many pies.


Finally, after many minutes, Robert was finished, and it was 6:00 sharp.


Robert brought out the dishes and they started digging in like hungry lions.


They managed to eat all the dishes presented to them. They were all stuffed to the max.


Robert hobbled over to the kitchen and pulled out a cart of pies. As Robert had said earlier, you eat until you are full, then eat a little bit more.


Robert also pulled out some toppings that go on pies, including whipped cream, ice cream, and lots more.


Robert, Kira, Jenna, Mom, and Dad were stuffed, (plus a little bit more).


Robert cleaned up the dishes and sent their parents on their way back home.


They were all still full a whole week later, a sign that they had eaten lots more than they should have.



          

Chapter 21: Christmas

It was that time of year again, the time where the christmas spirit was in the air.


Robert pulled out the lights and a large ladder. He walked into the doorway and started attaching lights to anything.


Robert plastered them to the ceiling and walls and floor and chandelier and buttons and switches and garage doors and lots more things around the entryway.


Robert pulled the extra wire to an outlet in the back of the garage. He plugged it into the switch and flipped it on.


ZZTT. the lights flickered off and the outlet produced a puff of smoke. Not good.


Robert pulled out a flashlight and unplugged the lights. He unscrewed the old socket, and replaced it with a more powerful one.


Robert walked over to the electrical box and replaced the breaker with a more powerful one.


Robert replugged in the lights and flicked the switch on.

The lights blinded Robert as he saw the beautiful lights twinkle.


Robert walked over to the living room, hauling a gigantic box of lights. Robert then walked out and grabbed the very large fake tree.


Robert called the girls over and they started putting up the tree. The tree was so large, that there was a treehouse inside of it.


They grabbed a string of lights, and loaded the light gun. They pulled the trigger and watched as the lights started covering the tree.


They kept pulling more and more lights through the gun, until the tree was completely covered.


They grabbed the extension cord and brought it to the closest hi- power outlet.


They flicked the switch and watched as over 500 strings of light lit up.


Robert then walked into the storage room again and pulled out some boxes of christmas ornaments.


This time, they grabbed a basket and flew around, hanging christmas ornaments in groups, instead of separating them out.


Robert finished up by picking up ornaments and separating them out to actually cover the tree.


Robert wove his way through the tree and onto the ladder to the treehouse.


Robert then grabbed a small tree and brought it up into the treehouse. He set it up and fished up another few strings of light.


Robert plugged in the lights and hung up the last few remaining ornaments, making sure to get it just right.


Robert then grabbed the girls and they got into the car to go to the store.


Robert then pushed them to go grab presents for everyone.


Robert ran off to go get everything the girls wanted that they were not getting at the very moment.


Robert raced around the store, trying to beat the girls and get to the car first.


Robert grabbed the most important thing; wrapping paper. He then ran around the store, grabbing things from cookies to TVs.


Robert boosted around the corner to the checkout lane, which of course was full.


Robert srinted over to the self- checkout, and he scanned the items as fast as the machine would “beep!”


Robert grabbed the bags and boxes and sprinted off to the car, slamming them in the first chest.


Robert calmed down and pulled out his phone to play some mind craft.


Meanwhile, Kira and Jenna were stuck in the lane of people waiting to check out, moving at a rate of 1 person per 5 minutes, and there were at least 10 people in front of them.

Kira was first to jump out of line and go to the crappy self- checkouts.


Jenna, meanwhile, was going to wait a bunch more. Kira was nowhere to be seen, as she was probably out in the car with Robert.


Jenna was getting bored, so she mind- made a man put 99% off candy for this 5 minutes up, and people came sprinting out of line to get the candy, which made Jenna next.


Jenna waited for a few minutes as she got checked out.


Jenna walked out, her hands full of toys and candy. She placed her stuff in the 3rd chest, next to Kira and Robert’s chest.


They got home and wrapped all of their presents and put them under the tree for tomorrow.


The next day, it was christmas, and Robert decided to get up early and pull a prank. Robert grabbed a pre- wrapped box and slid inside, and sure enough, 5 minutes later, the girls came over, and opened the box that he was in… “BOO!” Robert yelled, scaring the girls’ pants off, literally.


The girls pulled their pants back on as Robert laughed his head off. Again, literally.


Robert popped his head back on and they started opening presents.


The kids were all happy for the presents, as they played with all the things they had gotten, not even thinking about socializing.
